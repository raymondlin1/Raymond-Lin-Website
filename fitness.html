Fitness

In this section, I'll post my workout and fitness plans! I am by no means a bodybuilder or a fitness geek, but I do like to keep my body healthy. This section also helps me keep track of my workout and diet plans.

Workout Plan

I typically try to work out 3 times a week. Each day, I'll pick a particular group of exercises to focus on, such as push, pull or legs. In that group, I'll pick 2 muscle groups. For example, if I picked push, I can pick 2 muscle groups out of chests, triceps and shoulder. Then, for each muscle group, I'll pick 2 or 3 exercises to complete for that particular day. The exception is core and abs. At the end of each workout, I usually do a short core and ab section, unless I'm on a time crunch.

Push exercises typically encompass chests, triceps and shoulders. If you take a look at all these exercises, they all involve movements pushing weights away from the body.

Then I move onto the next exercise without rest. Completing all the target areas constitutes a set. Then, I take a 1 minute break. A typical ab session includes 3 sets.
