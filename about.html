Bio

I was born in New Zealand, before migrating to Sydney, Australia. Needless to say, I don't remember much about life in New Zealand, but I am still a proud kiwi! I spent the next part of my life in Sydney, where I happily grew up in a suburban area. From there, I moved to the Bay Area for middle school and high school. As a kid, I always enjoyed problem solving, such as doing puzzles and solving Rubik's Cubes, and building things, such as Lego structures. I was first introduced to the area of computer science in my senior year of high school, where I took AP Computer Science. I found programming to be very enjoyable, as I felt a great sense of satisfaction to work through a problem and finally solve it. Now, I am studying Computer Science as my academic major.
